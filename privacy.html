Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Heribase ("Heribase," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using Heribase, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect email address, login method, user ID or account identifier, subscription status, account settings, and preferences.

1.2 Collection and Item Information

When you use Heribase to identify, value, save, or track collectibles, we may collect photos or uploaded images, item names, categories, years, regions, descriptions, identification results, value estimates, price ranges, saved collection records, notes, tags, and other information you add.

1.3 Device and Usage Information

We may collect device model, operating system version, app version, language and region settings, IP address, device identifiers, log data, feature usage, pages viewed, crash reports, and performance data.

1.4 Purchase and Subscription Information

If you make a purchase or start a subscription through an app store or payment provider, we may receive product or subscription type, purchase status, renewal status, transaction identifier, and general payment-related status information. We do not collect or store your full payment card information.

1.5 Communications

If you contact us for support or feedback, we may collect your email address, message content, attachments you choose to provide, support history, and related communication records.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide, operate, and improve the Services; identify collectible items; generate and display value estimates; save and manage collection records; track price changes and market information; personalize your experience; process subscriptions and access rights; respond to support requests; analyze performance and usage; detect and address fraud, abuse, security issues, and technical problems; and comply with legal obligations.

3. Photos and Uploaded Content

Heribase may use photos and uploaded images you provide to deliver identification, valuation, collection management, and related features.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to upload and use any content you submit to the Services. Please do not upload images or content that you do not have permission to use.

We may process uploaded images using our own systems or trusted service providers to provide recognition, valuation, storage, analytics, or technical support.

4. Value Estimates and Collection Data

Heribase may generate value estimates, price ranges, and trend information based on available market references, historical data, collectible information, and other sources.

These estimates are for informational purposes only. They may not reflect the actual sale price, replacement value, insurance value, appraisal value, or future value of any item.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

5.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted vendors and service providers who help us operate the Services, such as cloud hosting providers, data storage providers, analytics providers, crash reporting tools, customer support tools, recognition or processing service providers, and payment or subscription infrastructure providers.

5.2 Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose information to comply with laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests; protect the rights, property, or safety of Heribase, our users, or others; detect or prevent fraud, abuse, security issues, or technical problems; or enforce our Terms of Use.

5.3 Business Transfers

If Heribase is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

6. Third-Party Services

The Services may include third-party tools, SDKs, or links. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Examples may include analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, subscription management, advertising attribution, or customer support tools.

7. Advertising and Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand how users interact with the Services and to improve product performance. If we use advertising, attribution, or tracking technologies, we will handle related data in accordance with applicable laws and platform requirements, and request permission where required.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and improve our Services.

You may request deletion of your account or certain personal information by contacting us at contactus@heritcoin.com. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business, security, fraud prevention, backup, or dispute resolution purposes.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, object to or restrict processing, withdraw consent, or request a copy of certain information. To exercise these rights, contact us at contactus@heritcoin.com.

11. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by contacting us at contactus@heritcoin.com or through any account deletion feature available in the app. After verification, we will delete or anonymize applicable personal information unless retention is required or permitted by law.

12. Children's Privacy

Heribase is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required by applicable law in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

13. International Data Transfers

Heribase may process and store information in countries or regions other than where you live. These locations may have data protection laws that differ from those in your jurisdiction. Where required, we take appropriate measures to protect personal information transferred internationally.

14. California and European Privacy Notices

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and related laws, including the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of certain sharing of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process personal information based on legal grounds such as performance of a contract, consent, legitimate interests, and compliance with legal obligations. You may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, request data portability, and lodge a complaint with a local data protection authority.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may provide additional notice through the Services or other appropriate means. Your continued use of the Services after an updated Privacy Policy becomes effective means that you acknowledge the updated policy.
