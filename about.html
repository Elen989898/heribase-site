About Heribase

Heribase helps collectors identify, value, organize, and track their collectibles in one place.

Identification Value estimates Collection records Price tracking

Our Mission

We built Heribase for people who want a clearer and more structured way to understand the items they own. Whether you are checking a coin, banknote, stamp, watch, or another collectible, Heribase gives you tools to record key details, view estimated value ranges, save items to your collection, and follow price changes over time.

Our mission is to make collecting easier to understand, easier to manage, and easier to preserve.

Collectibles often carry historical, cultural, and personal value, but information about them can be fragmented, inconsistent, or difficult to compare. Heribase brings identification, valuation references, collection management, and price tracking into a simple digital experience.

1

Identify Collectibles

Take a photo or upload an image to recognize collectible items and view available details such as name, category, year, issuing region, and other related information.

2

View Value Estimates

Heribase provides estimated value ranges based on available market references, historical data, and collectible information. These estimates help users better understand possible market value, not guarantee a sale price.

3

Organize Your Collection

Save items into your personal collection and keep everything organized in one place. You can review, update, and manage your collection records anytime.

4

Track Price Changes

Follow value changes and market trends over time, so you can better understand how your collectibles may move in the market.

5

Who Heribase Is For

Heribase is designed for collectors of different experience levels, including beginners, hobbyists, and experienced collectors who want a more convenient way to manage and understand their collections.

Our Approach

We focus on clarity, usability, and continuous improvement. Heribase is built to support multiple collectible categories and will continue improving its recognition, valuation, collection management, and price tracking experience.

Important Note

Reference tools, not guarantees.

Heribase provides information, estimates, and organizational tools for reference purposes only. Value estimates may vary based on market conditions, item condition, rarity, demand, data availability, and other factors. Heribase does not guarantee the authenticity, grade, value, liquidity, or future price of any item.
